Hick's Law

Amazon

Amazon

Hick's Law is a simple idea that says that the more choices you present your users with, the longer it will take them to reach a decision.Amazon has several windows with a large number of products to choose from where the user can enter wanting to buy a product and end up buying more.

Fitt's Law

Spotify

Spotify

Fitts' Law suggests that the larger and closer a target is, the faster and more accurately users can interact with it.And as we can see spotify is no exception, the songs, playlists and recommendations take up a lot of screen space and this helps the user to have more ease in making a decision and interacting.
