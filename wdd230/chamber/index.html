Guayaquil City Story

Guayaquil was founded on 25 July 1538 by Spanish conqueror Francisco de Orellana in the location of a native village and given the name Muy Noble y Muy Leal Ciudad de Santiago de Guayaquil ("Most Noble and Most Loyal City of Santiago de Guayaquil").

Location

Ecuador is a country equidistant between the two western extremes of the American continent. Guayaquil, its main commercial port, is located within the gulf of the same name, being the most important geographical point of the west coast of South America.
