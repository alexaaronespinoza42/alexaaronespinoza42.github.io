Explore Academic Excellence at NOA

At Northwestern Oasis Academy, we take pride in providing an exceptional educational experience that propels our students towards success. With a wide range of high-quality academic programs, from engineering to liberal arts, our students have the opportunity to explore their passions and push their boundaries.Our state-of-the-art campus boasts modern facilities, including cutting-edge laboratories, extensive libraries, equipped art studios, and top-notch sports facilities. Additionally, our students have access to a variety of extracurricular resources, such as student clubs, cultural events, and community service opportunities.

In our Academy, we pride ourselves on our academic and extracurricular achievements. Our faculty members are leaders in their fields and are committed to excellence in teaching and research. Our students have been recognized nationally and internationally for their academic achievements, innovative research, and contributions to the community.

Volunteer and Community Service Programs at NOA

At Northwestern Oasis Academy, we strongly believe in the importance of positively contributing to the community and fostering a sense of social responsibility among our students. Our volunteer and community service programs provide students with the opportunity to make a real difference in society while gaining invaluable experience outside the classroom.

Our students have access to a wide variety of volunteer opportunities in collaboration with local, national, and international organizations. From mentoring projects in local schools to park clean-up programs and housing construction projects, there are options for every interest and skill level.

Additionally, at Northwestern Oasis Academy, we encourage active participation of our students in university-organized community service initiatives. These projects are designed to address significant social issues and provide sustainable long-term solutions. Some examples include childhood literacy programs, health and wellness awareness campaigns , and community development projects in underserved areas.
